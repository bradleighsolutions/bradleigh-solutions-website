BradLeigh Solutions is an Alberta based vehicle transport company with over a decade on the road and a team that genuinely cares about the work they do.

Where It All Started

20 Years in the Making

BradLeigh Solutions was founded by Cam Watt, a trucking industry veteran whose roots in the business run deep. Before launching BradLeigh, Cam built and operated Advanced Coolant Technologies, a company specializing in antifreeze recycling and bulk fluid delivery across Western Canada. Managing a fleet of trucks and serving customers province wide, Cam developed a thorough understanding of what it takes to run a reliable, professional transport operation.

That foundation translated directly into BradLeigh Solutions, which Cam established over 11 years ago to serve the growing demand for professional vehicle and car hauling services across Canada and into the United States. From day one, the focus has been simple: show up, do the job right, and be the kind of company people can count on.

Based in Alberta and licensed to operate on both sides of the border, BradLeigh has grown into a trusted name for dealerships, fleet operators, and commercial customers who need vehicles moved safely, efficiently, and on time.
