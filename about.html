Where It All Started

29 Years in the Making

BradLeigh Solutions was founded by Cam Watt, a trucking industry veteran with over 29 years of hands on experience building and operating transport businesses across Western Canada. Long before BradLeigh, Cam was already managing fleets, serving commercial customers province wide, and developing the operational expertise that comes only from years of doing the work himself.

That deep industry foundation translated directly into BradLeigh Solutions, which Cam established over 11 years ago to serve the growing demand for professional vehicle and car hauling services across Canada and into the United States. From day one, the focus has been simple: show up, do the job right, and be the kind of company people can count on.

Based in Alberta and licensed to operate on both sides of the border, BradLeigh has grown into a trusted name for dealerships, fleet operators, and commercial customers who need vehicles moved safely, efficiently, and on time.
